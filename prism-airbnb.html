Prism red on Airbnb structure

Same Airbnb component vocabulary as the previous pass — Manrope, near-true-gray neutrals, white canvas, 8/12/16 radii, barely-there shadows, sentence-case CTAs, floating-label inputs, pill-search hero, photo-grid listing detail, 2-month range picker, guest stepper, social-login stack, sticky booking card, host card with Superhost badge. Only the Rausch role swaps to Prism brand red #D21905. Hover and pressed states cascade to Clay Red Light #AB342B and Clay Red Dark #810100, giving the gradient a Prism-family sunset feel. Charcoal stays charcoal for tab underlines, segment indicators, and focus rings — the red shows up at the brand-pause moments (heart, primary CTA, Superhost crown, save). Compare with the Components tab to see what Prism's own neutrals + Switzer would do to this same scaffolding.
