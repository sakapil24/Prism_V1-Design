Airbnb essence · Prism colors

Airbnb's structural language — Manrope, generous radii, near-true-gray neutrals, sentence-case CTAs, barely-there shadows — carrying Prism's locked palette where Rausch and friends would go. This page compares the two systems, names which Prism token plays each Airbnb role, and demonstrates the synthesis in component form. The hero search pill and Airbnb-custom illustrations are intentionally absent; icons are line-style per design.md §6.1.

Two systems, side by side

Role mapping

Which Prism token plays which Airbnb role

The merge isn't 1:1. Airbnb leans on three brand accents (Rausch, Babu, Arches); Prism leans on two reds (brand red + clay). Babu has no direct Prism analog and is dropped in favor of Prism's status palette. Arches collapses into clay-dark for hover states.

Role Airbnb Prism Notes
Primary CTA · brand red Rausch · #FF5A5F →Brand red · #D21905 Prism's red is deeper, less coral. Reads more deliberate.
CTA hover / pressed Rausch dim · #E0484D →Clay dark · #810100 Drops one full step rather than a 5% dim. Stronger affordance.
Hero gradient sweep Pink-red sunset →Prism sunset Same arc, our hue. Used on round CTAs and reserve buttons.
Soft accent · link Arches · #FC642D →Clay light · #AB342B Arches' orange has no equivalent. Clay light absorbs the role.

Aesthetic takeaways

What carries over from Airbnb

Type, geometry, weight. The Airbnb feel comes from the spacing between forms more than from any single token.

Typeface
Manrope — geometric, friendly, dense-friendly. 800-weight headlines tightly tracked at -0.025em are the signature.
Headlines
Centered, oversized (40–48px), sentence-case. They feel editorial without going serif.
CTA voice
Sentence-case (Save, Reserve) — not Prism's uppercase tracked CTAs. The Airbnb feel evaporates if you uppercase.
Radii
8 / 12 / 16px ramp. Buttons 8, cards 12, modals 16. Photo grid wraps at 16. No pill buttons except for filter chips.
Shadows
Three layers max, all under 0.12 alpha. Cards rest with no shadow — borders do the work.
Neutrals
Near-true-gray. #EBEBEB for borders, #717171 for muted text. Warmer than Prism's slate ramp — kept to preserve the feel.
Save icon
Heart (outline → red fill), not bookmark. Outline carries a 1.2px black stroke at low alpha to read on any photo.
Input pattern
Floating-label, 56px tall, animated. Active border thickens to 2px in charcoal.
What's out
Hero search pill, property-type filter strip, all Airbnb-illustrated icons (Belo, balloon, cabin, fire, etc.). Lucide line icons substitute.
Buttons

Canonical buttons — sentence-case, 8px radius

Primary uses solid Prism red. The gradient variant is reserved for hero / reserve moments — Airbnb's "sunset" CTA in Prism hue. Secondary is white with a strong charcoal border (Airbnb hallmark). Ghost is plain underlined text.

Form inputs

Floating-label inputs — Airbnb's hallmark

56px tall. Label sits centered until the field gains focus or content, then snaps to the top-left as a 11px caption. Active border is 2px charcoal — focus ring is implied by the thicker border. Stacked groups (login email + password) share borders to read as one block.

What this page is

A synthesis of Airbnb's product aesthetic with Prism's locked palette and design rules. It documents (1) the brand-color comparison, (2) the Prism→Airbnb role mapping, (3) the structural takeaways, and (4) the component patterns that travel between the two systems. The hero search pill and Airbnb-custom icons are intentionally absent. For Prism's canonical tokens and rules, see design.md.
